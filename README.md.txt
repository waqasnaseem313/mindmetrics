### üìä **Supported Assessment Scales**
- **Perceived Stress Scale (PSS-10)** - Measures perceived stress
- **GAD-7 Anxiety Scale** - Generalized anxiety disorder screening
- **PHQ-9 Depression Scale** - Depression severity assessment
- **DASS-21** - Depression, anxiety, and stress scales
- **Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (EPDS)**
- **AUDIT** - Alcohol use disorders identification
- **Kessler Psychological Distress Scale (K10)**
- **PAD Emotion Scales** - Pleasure, arousal, dominance
- **Profile of Mood States (POMS)**
- **PANAS** - Positive and negative affect schedule
- **SPANE** - Scale of positive and negative experience
- **MAACL-R Affect Checklist**
- **BRUMS** - Brunel mood scale for athletes

### üöÄ **Key Features**
- **Single Participant Assessment** - Individual scale administration
- **Batch Processing** - Upload CSV files for multiple participants
- **Detailed Reports** - Generate PDF and DOCX reports
- **Real-time Scoring** - Automatic calculation and interpretation
- **Professional Interpretation** - Severity levels and recommendations
- **Responsive Design** - Works on desktop and mobile
